What's On

Dimensions by Artie G. T.

April 5,2025 - July 5, 2025

"Boundless Dimensions" invites viewers to explore the fusion of emotions, technology, and abstraction. Artie G. T. transforms universal themes into expressive works, inspiring curiosity and personal interpretation.

Stories in Strokes by Linea Verve

August 5, 2025 – November 5, 2025

„Stories in Strokes“ celebrates simplicity and expression through hand-drawn sketches by Linea Verve. Each artwork captures raw emotions and ideas with minimal lines, leaving space for imagination and interpretation.

Fruitscapes by Chroma Terra

August 5, 2025 – November 5, 2025

„Fruitscapes“ transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary. Chroma Terra captures the essence of bananas and persimmons through abstract, textured compositions that celebrate the beauty of simplicity and form.

Tides and Tales by Marin A. Brush

December 5, 2025 – March 5, 2026

„Tides and Tales“ explores the resilience and solitude of seafarers, brought to life by Marin A. Brush’s expressive oil paintings. The artworks capture the moods of the sea and its people with rich textures and earthy tones.

How to Get Here

By Public Transport

Metro: Take the Blue Line to Creative Square Station (5-minute walk to the gallery).

Bus: Routes 21, 45, and 89 stop at Art District North.

Train: The nearest station is Metropolis Central, connected via Metro or a 10-minute taxi ride.

By Car

From Downtown: Head east on Main Street, turn right onto Creative Avenue. The gallery is on the left side after 2 blocks.

From the Highway: Take Exit 23 towards Art District, follow the signs to Creative Avenue.
